TexTalk
-------

The TexTalk project was undertaken as a thesis topic by Ben Wooller of the
University of Queensland in 1998. It is an English text to speech system
written in Visual C++, and it utilises the MBROLA synthesiser.

Acknowledgements:
Julie Vonwiller for phoneme duration data

References:
Wasser, J.A., English to Phoneme Translation, Littleton: Digital Equipment
Corp., 1985.
(for English letter to sound rules)
